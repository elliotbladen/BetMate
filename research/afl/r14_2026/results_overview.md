## One-liner Summaries

**1. Adelaide def Western Bulldogs by 57 (121-64)**
Crows exploded with a nine-goal first quarter that ended all contest; Jordan Dawson (32 disp, 3 goals) and returning Riley Thilthorpe led a statement win that reignited Adelaide's top-four push.

**2. Geelong def Gold Coast by 45 (105-60)**
White Out night at GMHBA — Cats rode a seven-goal second-quarter blitz after a slow start; Jeremy Cameron booted three, Gold Coast's execution inside 50 was poor, and Hardwick conceded "ridiculous" missed chances.

**3. Melbourne def Essendon by 45 (95-50)**
Dominant stat win: Melbourne +22 inside 50s, +49 disposals, +11 clearances, +33 contested possessions. Kysaiah Pickett (32 disp, 3 goals, 10 score involvements) was best afield. Essendon clawed back in Q3 before Melbourne pulled clear in Q4.

**4. North Melbourne def West Coast by 1 (74-73)**
Thriller at Optus — North led by 20 in Q4 but West Coast kicked the last three goals; Jake Waterman hit the post from 30m with 45 seconds left and Nick Larkey's consolation after the siren doubled as the game-winning moment. Jack Darling (ex-Eagle) kicked four first-half goals.

**5. Sydney def Port Adelaide by 3 (93-90)**
Sydney trailed by nine at three-quarter time, then kicked five goals to three in a stunning final quarter comeback. Port led 13.12 to 10.11 at the last change — seven behinds in the first half cost the Power dearly. Swans join equal top with Fremantle.

**6. Brisbane def Richmond by 35 (115-80)**
Lions led by just three at half-time in Hobart before Will Ashcroft (38 disp, 2 goals, 8 clearances) and Lachie Neale dominated Q3 in a seven-goal-to-three blitz. Eric Hipwood (200th game) and Logan Morris (3 goals) added class; Jasper Alger (4 goals) was Richmond's lone bright spot.

**7. St Kilda def GWS Giants by 8 (96-88)**
Saints knocked off $2.08 favourites in a scrappy game. Nasiah Wanganeen-Milera (32 disp, 775m gained) was electric; Giants couldn't contain the Saints' outside game. Stephen Coniglio missed with hamstring concerns; GWS now face a horror injury list heading into R15.
